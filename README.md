# Private Markets Glossary

This repository provides a neutral, structured glossary of terms commonly used in private markets, allocator research, fund documentation, and institutional investment analysis.

It is designed for use by:

- Researchers and analysts
- Allocators and investment teams
- Fund managers and advisors
- Data, analytics, and AI teams
- Documentation and schema designers

Definitions reflect commonly observed usage across institutional disclosures, regulatory filings, and public investment materials.
