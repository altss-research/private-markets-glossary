# Allocator Concepts

## Allocator
An entity responsible for deploying capital across investment strategies on behalf of beneficiaries or stakeholders.

## LP (Limited Partner)
An investor providing capital to a fund with limited liability and no role in day-to-day management.

## Investment Committee
Group responsible for approving investment decisions based on risk, return, and strategic fit.
